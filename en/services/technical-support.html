Frequently asked questions

What is included in warranty support?

Within 1 month after delivery, I will correct bugs and minor defects related to the work done free of charge.

How does monthly support work?

You choose a plan (basic or full). I monitor the site, make updates and make minor adjustments as part of the plan.

Can you maintain a WordPress site?

Yes, I support WordPress, PrestaShop and Laravel projects — core updates, plugins, security.

How quickly do you respond to a problem?

Critical problems (site not working) are solved within a few hours. Non-critical - within 1-2 working days.
