Development of online stores

I develop fully functional E-commerce solutions — from small stores on ready-made platforms to large-scale custom projects on Laravel with their own admin panel.

Platforms and technologies

Depending on the budget, scale and specifics of your business, I offer solutions on different platforms. For small businesses - PrestaShop or Woo Commerce, for medium and large - a custom shop on Laravel with Filament PHP, which gives maximum flexibility and control.

I design each store with conversion in mind: easy navigation, fast loading, optimized product page and simplified checkout process — all this directly affects sales.

PrestaShop vs Laravel - how to choose?

PrestaShop or Woo Commerce — an ideal choice for a start. Ready ecosystem of modules, fast deployment, low cost. Suitable for stores up to 10,000 SKUs.

Custom store on Laravel — the choice for those who want complete control over logic, performance and scaling. No platform limitations, unique business logic, speed of work.

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to develop an online store?

Store on PrestaShop/WooCommerce — from $500. Custom on Laravel — from $1,500. The exact price depends on the number of features.

What payment systems can be connected?

LiqPay, WayForPay, Fondy, Stripe, NovaPay, PayPal — any of your choice. You can also connect several at the same time.

Is it possible to integrate Nova Poshta?

Yes, I connect Nova Poshta's API: autosubstitution of branches, calculation of delivery cost, creation of TTN.

Will there be an admin panel for product management?

Necessarily. On Laravel is Filament PHP with full product, order and customer management. PrestaShop/WooCommerce has a built-in admin.
