Platforms & Technologies

Depending on the budget, scale, and specifics of your business, I offer solutions on various platforms. For small businesses — PrestaShop or WooCommerce, for medium/large — a custom Laravel store with Filament PHP for ultimate flexibility and control.

PrestaShop vs Laravel — how to choose?

PrestaShop або Woo Commerce — ideal choice for starting. Ready ecosystem of modules, fast deployment, low cost. Suitable for stores up to 10,000 SKUs.

Custom Laravel Store — choice for those who want full control over logic, performance, and scalability. No platform limits, unique business logic, high speed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an online store cost?

Store on PrestaShop/WooCommerce — from $500. Custom on Laravel — from $1500. Exact price depends on the number of features.

What payment systems can be integrated?

Stripe, PayPal, Braintree, and any local systems of your choice. Multiple can be connected simultaneously.

Can you integrate shipping APIs?

Yes, I connect shipping APIs: automatic branch selection, shipping cost calculation, and label creation.

Will there be an admin panel to manage products?

Absolutely. On Laravel — it is Filament PHP with full management of products, orders, and clients. On PrestaShop/WooCommerce — the built-in admin panel.
