CRM and ERP systems

I develop turnkey corporate management systems: from a simple CRM for the sales department to a full-fledged ERP system for automating the entire business.

Why own CRM and not ready?

Ready-made CRMs (Salesforce, Bitrix24) are universal, but overloaded with excess and cost every month. A proprietary system is exactly your logic, without unnecessary modules, without monthly payments and with full control over data.

I am building a CRM/ERP on the basis Filament PHP + Laravel is a powerful stack that allows you to quickly create complex admin panels with a beautiful interface and flexible logic.

What I implement in CRM/ERP

  • ✓
    Customer management — customer base, deals, sales funnel, reminders.
  • ✓
    Order management — reception, processing, statuses, customer notifications.
  • ✓
    Personnel management — roles, schedules, tasks, KPIs.
  • ✓
    Reporting and analytics — dashboards, graphs, filters, export to Excel/PDF.
  • ✓
    Automation — trigger actions, email/SMS notifications, task planners.
  • ✓
    Integration — connection to the site, 1C, Nova Poshta, Telegram, payment gateways.
  • ✓
    Rights management — roles, permissions, access restrictions for different departments.

Frequently asked questions

How is your own CRM better than Bitrix24 or Salesforce?

A proprietary system is exactly your logic, without unnecessary modules, without monthly payments and with full control over data. You pay once for development.

Yes, CRM connects to the site via an API. Orders, leads, customers — everything gets into the system automatically.

How long does it take to develop a CRM?

Simple CRM for sales - 2-4 weeks. Complex ERP with several modules — from 2 months.

Can I add new modules later?

Yes, the system is designed modularly. You can always expand it with new functions without rework.
